# GamerPulse

A personal game tracker that lives on your desktop. It automatically finds the games you have installed, tracks how long you play them, and gives you a clear picture of your gaming habits — all stored locally, no account required.

---

## What it does

### Finds your games automatically

GamerPulse scans your PC for installed games across every major platform — Steam, Epic Games, GOG, Xbox Game Pass, Riot Games, Ubisoft Connect, EA App, and Battle.net. Open the app and your library is already there. You can also point it at any folder on your PC and it will pick up games that aren't tied to a launcher.

### Tracks your playtime in the background

Once a game is in your library, GamerPulse quietly watches for it running in the background. When you start playing, it starts a session. When you close the game, the session ends. You don't have to do anything.

### Shows you your history

Every session is saved. You can see exactly when you played, for how long, and how that breaks down over days, weeks, and months. A heatmap shows your activity at a glance — the same kind you'd see on GitHub, but for gaming.

### Streaks and achievements

GamerPulse tracks how many days in a row you've gamed and highlights milestones — your longest streak, first 10-hour session, most played game, and more.

### Goals

Set a playtime goal for any game — weekly, monthly, or total. The app tracks your progress and lets you know how close you are.

### Your library, your way

- **Favorites** — pin games to the top of your library
- **Tags** — organize games however you like
- **Notes** — write anything you want on a game's page
- **Custom covers** — search for and set your own cover art
- **Manual entries** — add games that aren't detected automatically

### Game History

Removing a game from your library doesn't delete your playtime data. Removed games live in a History page where their session records are preserved. You can permanently delete a game from there if you want it gone for good — and it won't come back on the next scan.

### Themes

Comes with Catppuccin, Dracula, and Nord themes out of the box. Every color in the interface is customizable, and you can save your own themes by name.

### Stays out of your way

- Minimizes to the system tray so it's always running without cluttering your taskbar
- Scans for new games automatically when you open the app, or on a schedule you set
- Launches games directly from inside the app

---

## Privacy

Everything is stored on your machine in a single database file. Nothing is sent anywhere. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no account (for now).
